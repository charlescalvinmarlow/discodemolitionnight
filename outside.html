The doubleheader sold out, leaving at least 20,000 people outside the ballpark.

Some leapt turnstiles, climbed fences, and entered through open windows.

[The promotions director] received word that thousands of people were trying to get into the park without tickets and sent his security personnel to the stadium gates to stop them.

Attendees were supposed to deposit their records into a large box, some 4 by 6 by 5 feet (1.2 by 1.8 by 1.5 m) tall; once the box was overflowing, many people brought their discs to their seats.
